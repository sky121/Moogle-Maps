Proposal

Summary of the Project

We will generate a start and end coordinate in the map. The starting point will be on ground level. The ending point being some coordinate 1000 blocks, not including verticality, away from the starting point. Steve will try to find the fastest achievable path within a time constraint that minimizes the time taken while he is on his way to the destination. During the journey, Steve might need to build paths and vessels to travel across different terrain. The agent will take in the coordinates of the end point, its current position, health, hunger, and the blocks it can see in order to take the best movement action to reach the goal. Movement actions include going forward and backwards, sprinting forwards, rotating the body left and right, pitching head up and down, jumping, breaking block, placing block, crafting basic objects, eating food, and interacting with objects.

AI/ML Algorithms

We plan on implementing reinforcement learning using a neural network.

Evaluation Plan

We will be evaluating the success of our project by using a metric of the time it takes to reach the destination or the distance of the agent from the endpoint, if it fails to make it. The baseline for our agent is to move closer to the destination coordinate under time limit without dying (falling, lava, drowning). We expect to see improvements from just random actions to movements toward the destination coordinate while avoiding natural obstacles that will slow its rate of approach, maintaining hunger levels, and utilizing sprinting. In order to understand the growth of our agent, we will be graphing the agents distance from the destination coordinate and the time it takes to reach the destination as well. We will also monitor the agent's pathing by graphings its coordinates on a 2d plane to see how well the agent is doing and if it is improving from just taking random paths. Through this we can visualize the internals of our algorithms to see how well our agent is learning. For the moonshot case, we would like it if the agent started building pathways or utilizing boats to go over the difficult terrain rather than avoiding them entirely. Additionally, we would l ike to see our agent using sprinting and food to reduce the time it takes to reach the destination.
